# HSR_Motors_CRM_Dashboard
CRM Dashboard UI for HSR Motors. a modern lead management and sales tracking system designed to help sales teams manage customer leads, follow-ups, and dealership analytics efficiently.

Overview

The application helps sales teams manage customer leads received from multiple platforms such as Facebook, Google, websites, and offline events. It replaces manual spreadsheet tracking with a clean and user-friendly dashboard experience.

Features
Dashboard with lead statistics
Lead listing and status tracking
Search functionality for quick lead access
Lead details management screen
AI lead score and automation section
Follow-up and duplicate lead reminders

Screens Included
Dashboard Screen
Lead Listing Screen
Lead Details Screen
AI Automation Section

Design Approach
The UI was designed with a clean and modern layout focused on usability, quick navigation, and efficient lead management for sales teams and business managers.

Tools Used
Figma

Project Type
